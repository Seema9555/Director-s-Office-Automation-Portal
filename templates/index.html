About The Portal

Digitizing the Future of
Academic Administration

The Director's Portal is an initiative by UIET CSJMU to transition from manual, paper-based workflows to a secure, high-speed digital ecosystem. We aim to provide transparency, reduce processing time, and ensure data integrity for all stakeholders.

Accelerated Processing

Requests move instantly through our 2-Tier system (APEC Verification → Director Approval), cutting delays by 90%.

Tamper-Proof Security

Every issued certificate is embedded with a unique QR code and cryptographic hash, making forgery impossible.

100% Paperless

Contributing to a greener campus by eliminating physical forms and manual record-keeping.
